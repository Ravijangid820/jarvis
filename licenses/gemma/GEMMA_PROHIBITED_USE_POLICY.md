> Retrieved 2026-06-30. This Policy is incorporated by reference into the Gemma Terms of Use (§3.2).

You may **not** use Gemma or its Model Derivatives, and may not allow others to use them, to:

**1. Perform or facilitate dangerous, illegal, or malicious activities**, including:
- Child sexual abuse or exploitation; generating or distributing CSAM.
- Facilitating illegal goods/services/activities (e.g. illegal drugs, weapons).
- Promoting or facilitating terrorism, violent extremism, or other serious crime.
- Unlicensed practice of regulated professions (legal, medical, financial, etc.).
- Attempts to override, circumvent, or defeat safety filters or otherwise misuse the model.

**2. Generate or distribute content that violates others' rights**, including infringing,
misappropriating, or otherwise violating any individual's or entity's rights.

**3. Generate harmful content**, including content that:
- Promotes or incites hatred, harassment, or violence against individuals or groups.
- Generates personally identifying information for harmful purposes.

**4. Generate deceptive or misleading content** intended to misinform, misrepresent, or mislead —
including impersonation, false claims of authorship/expertise, and defamatory content.

**5. Generate sexually explicit content** for pornographic or sexual-gratification purposes (bona fide
scientific, educational, documentary, or artistic uses excepted).

Google may update this Policy. Anyone redistributing Gemma must pass these restrictions through to
recipients as enforceable terms (Gemma Terms of Use §3.1, §3.2).
